## 🧠 The "Sensor-First" Analyst

I'm a Mechanical Engineer (Aviation Transport) with hands-on experience at **SmartLynx Airlines** and **AMAC Aerospace** — working across Airbus and Boeing fleets in EASA Part-M and Part-145 regulated environments.

I approach data the same way I approach aircraft systems: **precision, logic, and root-cause resolution.** Most analysts learn to work with data. I also know what the data *means* — what an Airworthiness Directive is, how MRO throughput flows, why a frozen pitot reading matters. That domain depth is what makes the difference between a dashboard and a decision.

This portfolio covers product analytics, SaaS metrics, marketing performance, and aviation data engineering — all built during an intensive data analytics course alongside my MSc research.
